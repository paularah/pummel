ABOUT US

Africa is the second largest landmass on earth. The culture and cuisine in many of our continent's countries are diverse as the people theemselves. Ranging from the arid Northern Africa to the safari Plains of Southern Africa, to Fine Land in Eastern Africa, down to the Tropical vegetation of Western Africa. There are several mouth-watering delicacies present Pummel X brings them all to your doorstep with style, elegance and convenience all at an affordable cost

  • Special attention paid to the quality of food materials.
  • Meticulous examination of meat source to ensure the absence of animal cruelty.
  • Prepaid under very hygeinic conditions
  • A approach to African cuisine, whilst still maintaning the traditional basics.
  • Avialabilty of Vegeterian menu options.
  • Fast, safe and effective delivery of orders.

The Above reasons are why PUMMEL X the best African Restaurant around and why you should dine with us
